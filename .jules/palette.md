## 2024-05-24 - Contextual Discoverability in Gradio UI
**Learning:** Global "wall-of-text" instructions at the top of a Gradio interface are often ignored by users and clutter the layout. However, Gradio components like `gr.Textbox` and `gr.Slider` support an `info` parameter that places helpful context directly under the input, vastly improving discoverability and maintaining a clean top-level layout. For components that lack `info` support (like `gr.File`), placing a small `gr.Markdown` directly above the component serves as an effective alternative to top-level instructions.
**Action:** When adding explanatory text or instructions to a Gradio interface, prioritize using the `info` parameter on individual components. If the component doesn't support it, use localized `gr.Markdown` directly adjacent to the component rather than placing all instructions at the top of the page.
